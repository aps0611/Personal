Decoding My Bookish Universe




Over the past few years. I've traversed the landscapes of imagination, diving into the vibrant narratives woven by authors from around the globe. With every book, I've sailed on ships of history, treaded the alleys of mystery, and soared through galaxies of science fiction. Each page added a layer to the tapestry of my thoughts, a mosaic of emotions and reflections that I've cherished. But now, I'm ready to unravel a new chapter in this story—an unconventional exploration into the heart of my reading preferences.

Today, once again as before, I have decided to don my literary detective hat.

And my esteemed client is none other than my cherished bookshelf itself, and the enigma that lies before me is the covert pattern concealed within its collection—a mystery waiting to be unraveled
