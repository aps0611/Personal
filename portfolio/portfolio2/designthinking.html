  • Project URL: article by Sowmya

The EAST paradigm is a comprehensive framework for designing human-centered products. It begins with empathizing with customers by creating personas and mapping their journey to understand their needs and pain points. The analysis phase involves delving deeper into customer problems and exploring different perspectives through multi-why questions and the conflict of interest approach. The solve phase employs brainstorming and TRIZ methods to generate and refine solutions. Finally, the test/prototype stage involves creating prototypes and testing them with users. The session provided interactive examples and videos, fostering engagement and resulting in the development of a solution that addressed personal and professional needs.
